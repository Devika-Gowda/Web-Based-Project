Repeat Breeders


Ingredients Required:-
 White radish
 Aloe Vera leaves
 Moringa leaves
 Cissus quadrangularis stem
 Curry leaves
 Turmeric

Step-wise treatment protocol :-
 1.Start the treatment on the first or second day of the heat cycle.
 2.Feed one full radish everyday for 5 days. Smear it with jaggery and salt before feeding.
 3.Feed one fresh leaf of Aloe Vera (after removing the thorns) once a day for 4 days along with jaggery and salt after step 2.
 4.Feed 4 handfuls of fresh Moringa leaves along with jaggery and salt once a day for the next 4 days after step 3.
 5.Feed 4 handfuls of fresh Cissus stem with jaggery and salt once a day for 4 days after step 4.
 6.Lastly, feed 4 handfuls of fresh curry leaves mixed with turmeric,jaggery and salt once a day for 4 days after step 5.
 7.All the herbs are to be given orally in fresh form, once a day only.
 8.Repeat the treatment once again if the animal has not convinced.
