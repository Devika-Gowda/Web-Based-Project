Uterine/rectal prolapse in bovines


Ingredients Required:-
 Aloe Vera gel from one big leaf
 Turmeric Powder - 1 pinch
 Mimosa Pudica(touch me not plant) leaves - 2 handfuls

Medicine Preparation:-
 1.Remove the gel from one big aloe vera leaf.
 2.Wash the gel multiple times in water till the slimeness is reduced.
 3.Add a pinch of turmeric to the gel and boil to half the volume and allow to cool.
 4.Blend two handfuls of mimosa pudica leaves to make a paste.

Method of application :-
 1.Wash the prolapsed mass thoroughly.
 2.Sprinkle the aloe vera gel on the mass thoroughly.
 3.Once the gel dries, apply the mimosa pudica leaf paste to the mass.
 4.Repeat the process 3-4 times a day till the condition resolves.
