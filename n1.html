Ingredients Required:-
 Aloe Vera-250g
 Turmeric powder-50g
 Calcium oxide(lime)-15g

Medicine Preparation(To be freshly prepared every day):-
 1.weigh 250 gram of freshly cut Aloe Vera.
 2.Slice the entire leaf(with sheath) into small pieces.
 3.Blend the cut pieces thoroughly.
 4.Add 50 grams(2 hand fulls) of turmeric powder.
 5.Add 3-4 teaspoons (15 g) of lime(calcium oxide).
 6.Blend till a reddish paste is obtained.

Method of application of the mastitis formulation on the affected animal:-
 1.Wash and scrub the under with clean water.
 2.Completely milk out all the quarters including the affected quarter.
 3.Take a handful of the paste.
 4.Add 150-200 ml water to make it watery.
 5.Apply thorougly on all areas of the udder.
 6.Repeat the procedure 10 times a day for 5 days.
The mixture should be freshly prepared daily. The last application for the day should be prepared in sesame or mustard oil. The udder should be wiped dry before application of the prepartion in oil. Allow the mixture split on the floor to remain.

Oral administration:-
 1.Slice 2 fresh lemons into halves.
 2.Place each half of lemon inside the mouth.
 3.Close the mouth and allow to chew.
 4.Repeat the process for each slice.
 5.Feed 2 sliced lemons at a time, twice daily for 3 days.

Additional formulation if there is blood in milk:-
 Curry leaves- 2 handfulls
 Jaggery - 100gm
 Blend the curry leaves and jaggery to form a paste.
Feed the paste orally twice daily till condition resolves.
